About AI in Clinical Research

Artificial Intelligence is transforming the clinical research and healthcare industry at an unprecedented pace — enabling faster drug discovery, smarter patient recruitment, and more accurate clinical data analysis.

AI tools support automation of clinical trial processes, help researchers analyse large datasets and electronic health records, and enable better patient outcomes with faster regulatory approvals.

Faster drug discovery and patient recruitment through AI-driven workflows

Automation of clinical trial processes and data management

AI tools for predictive modeling, risk monitoring, and safety surveillance

Enables faster regulatory approvals and better patient outcomes

Programme Overview

A 2-month intensive covering AI fundamentals through real-world clinical research applications

1

2-Month Intensive Programme

A focused, time-efficient programme designed to introduce AI applications specifically in clinical research and pharma.

2

AI & Machine Learning Fundamentals

Covers core concepts of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning as applied to pharmaceutical and healthcare settings.

3

AI in Clinical Trial Design & Management

Understanding how AI enhances clinical trial design, patient recruitment, and overall trial management processes.

4

Clinical Data Analysis Using AI Tools

Training on clinical data analysis and interpretation using modern AI tools, including EHR and large dataset analysis.

5

Real-World Data (RWD) & Real-World Evidence (RWE)

Introduction to RWD and RWE concepts, and how AI leverages real-world information for predictive analytics and decision support.

6

AI in Pharmacovigilance & Safety Monitoring

Understanding AI-driven approaches to drug safety surveillance, signal detection, and adverse event monitoring.

7

Case Studies & Industry Examples

Practical learning through real-world case studies demonstrating AI applications across clinical research sectors.

8

Regulatory Considerations for AI in Healthcare

Guidance on the evolving global regulatory landscape governing the use of AI tools in healthcare and clinical research.
